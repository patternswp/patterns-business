Patterns Business is a versatile block-based WordPress theme designed for businesses, agencies, startups, and corporate professionals. It offers the tools needed to create a polished and impactful online presence.

== Description ==

Patterns Business is designed for businesses, agencies, startups, and corporate professionals. Built with WordPress Full Site Editing (FSE), this theme allows you to customize headers, footers, templates, and global styles effortlessly.

The theme includes pre-designed patterns specifically crafted for showcasing services, portfolios, team sections, testimonials, and contact pages. Its responsive design ensures your website looks professional and functional on all devices.

Whether you are an entrepreneur, agency, or corporate professional, Patterns Business provides a flexible and powerful platform to elevate your brand and connect with your audience effectively.
